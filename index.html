A new pre-paid debit card

The Medway Credit Union is able to provide members with a pre-paid Visa CredEcard card which is very versatile. You may pay money onto it - wages, benefits, share withdrawals or loans from the Credit Union. You can also transfer money from your bank account onto the card.

The Credecard can be used in any shop that accepts Visa at home or abroad. A range of shops give rewards in the form of cash back.

There are many other advantages to having a CredEcard, please enquire at the office.
